Introduction

About Kaadu UI

Kaadu UI is a theme-aware, nature-inspired UI design system. It features blur effects, console-style typography, and components with green aesthetics. The system is designed for clarity, accessibility, and a modern look, making it ideal for applications that want a fresh, natural feel.

Its purpose is to provide a consistent, visually appealing set of controls and layouts that adapt to both light and dark themes, with a focus on usability and style.

Styled Controls

Text Box

Styled with rounded corners, subtle borders, and background color that adapts to the theme. Focused state highlights the border in green.

Number Box

Similar to the text box, but for numeric input. No spinner controls for a clean look. Focused state uses the theme's green color.

Button

Buttons have a soft background, rounded corners, and bold font. Hover and active states use green highlights for feedback.

Link

Links are styled with green color and rounded background on hover, blending with the theme. Active state dims the color.

Timeline List

The timeline list visually represents sequential stages or events, styled with a vertical line and highlighted markers for each step. It is ideal for showing progressions, processes, or life cycles in a clear, accessible format.

Example: Evolution of Plants

  • Single-celled Algae: Origin of photosynthetic life
  • Multicellular Algae: Simple colonies and filaments
  • Bryophytes: First land plants (mosses, liverworts)
  • Pteridophytes: Vascular plants (ferns, horsetails)
  • Gymnosperms: Seed plants without flowers (conifers, cycads)
  • Angiosperms: Flowering plants with enclosed seeds

Custom Controls

Combo Box

The combo box features a modern dropdown with blur and green highlights. Options are styled for clarity and accessibility.

Radial Progress

Radial progress indicators are circular, animated components that visually represent progress or completion. They feature a smooth, green highlight that fills the circle, providing a clear, accessible way to show ongoing activity or status.

Expander

Expanders are collapsible panels with animated transitions, rounded corners, and a clean, modern look. The header uses bold text and the panel background adapts to the theme.

Popup

Popups use a blurred, semi-transparent background overlay and a centered panel with rounded corners. The popup content is clearly separated and accessible.

Cards

Cards are distinct, clickable containers with rounded corners and adaptive backgrounds. They are perfect for organizing related content, presenting information, or grouping actions in a visually appealing and accessible way.

Loading Progressbar

The loading progress bar offers a clear, animated indication of ongoing activity. Its smooth green highlights integrate with the theme, making progress easy to notice without being distracting. This ensures users always know when something is loading.
